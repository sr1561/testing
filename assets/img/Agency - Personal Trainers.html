Services

Following are differnt practice options

Personal training

1:1 training sessions: just you and your personal trainer
Injury minimisation and prevention of evasive movement through trained control
Place and time will be adapted to your needs

Group training

We like to keep our group training classes to a 6:1 ratio of clients to coaches. Our classes typically run 8-12 clients and have 2 coaches. This ratio allows for optimal coaching.

Remote workout

live streaming workouts is a great alternative to coming into the studio. It is a live workout with a personal trainer leading the workout, demonstrating the exercises and providing alternatives as needed. It is as close to having a personal trainer in the room with you as you can get.
